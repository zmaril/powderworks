# Design notes

The flagship's job is delegation: members audit themselves, the captain
checks the auditors, and policy divergence is surfaced rather than silently
resolved. The full reasoning lives in housekeeping's
[design doc](https://github.com/zmaril/housekeeping/blob/main/notes/design.md);
this file is for what's particular to the flagship — the roster's shape, the
site to come at powderworks.dev, and whatever is being mulled before it's
policy.

Current policy thinking: every open source member carries notes/design.md
(one place to look for a repo's design brain), enforced below in
housecaptain.toml as a required file rather than a suggestion.
